TITLE
*****

MIUI Center Clock (unofficial)

SHORT DESCRIPTION
*****************

Center Clock let's you easily change the look of your status bar under MIUI.

FULL DESCRIPTION
****************

This app requires ROOT!

It downloads and applies the popular Center Clock Mod from mi-globe.com. It will display the clock in the middle of the status bar like on iOS.


<b>DISCLAIMER:</b>
This app is unofficial and not linked to MIUI or Xiaomi.


<b>Features:</b>
★Enable/disable Center Clock Mod
★works on-the-fly without reboot
★Mod is downloaded directly from mi-globe.com
★Other mods (like Beauty Mod) are preserved

<b>Prerequisites:</b>
★MIUI 8 ROM installed (Beta versions are not supported)
★Device is rooted
